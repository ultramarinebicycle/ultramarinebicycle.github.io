Cabin in the Woods

As 2021 was drawing to a close, I wanted to end a year of experimenting on a high note. What better way to do that than make a light-hearted game for me and my friends? A warm cabin in the woods, with a fireplace and a smoke-puffing chimney, as well as a frozen lake and snowfall outside. I think that completed the experience.
